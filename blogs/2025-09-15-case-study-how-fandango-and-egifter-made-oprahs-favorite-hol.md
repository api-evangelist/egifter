<p style="font-size: 18px;">When Oprah revealed The Color Purple as her favorite holiday gift of the year, she wasn’t talking about a simple email code. She wanted fans to experience it as a real gift—something you could hold, place under a tree, and unwrap.</p>
<blockquote style="font-size: 18px;">
<p>“I said, I want it to come in a box. With a bow. So it feels like a real gift.”<br />
— Oprah, via Instagram reel.</p>
</blockquote>
<p style="font-size: 18px;">Fandango had the movie. Oprah had the moment. What they needed was a way to deliver that vision—fast, beautifully, and at scale. They needed more than a ticketing platform. They needed a digital-to-physical gifting solution that felt joyful, personal, and seamless.</p>
<p style="font-size: 18px;">That’s where eGifter Merchants came in.</p>

<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Delivery by email, text, or in a custom gift box with purple ribbon</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">An elegant gifting experience—even at the last minute</span></li>
