</div><div class="fusion-text fusion-text-51"><p><b style="font-size: 18px;">HUNTINGTON, N.Y., OCT 7, 2025</b><span style="font-size: 18px;"> (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) — </span><span style="background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);"><span style="font-size: 18px;">eGifter, the gift card technology company pioneering </span><a href="https://egifter.ai/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><b style="font-size: 18px;">AI for the gift card industry</b></a><span style="font-size: 18px;">, today announced two new AI products: </span><b style="font-size: 18px;">eGifter AI Personalization™</b><span style="font-size: 18px;">, which lets shoppers generate digital images to accompany eGift cards, and </span><b style="font-size: 18px;">eGifter AI SmartScreen™</b><span style="font-size: 18px;">, which automatically reviews customer-submitted content for brand-appropriate material.</span></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="padding-bottom: 20px;"><b style="font-size: 18px;">eGifter AI Personalization:</b><span style="font-size: 18px;"> Uses generative AI to turn shopper prompts into custom image designs that accompany digital gift cards. The personalized image is delivered with the eGift card, adding a visual, emotional touch to birthdays, holidays, corporate gifts, and more. No design skills needed—just type a few words and preview the result in seconds.</span></li>
<li><b style="font-size: 18px;">eGifter AI SmartScreen:</b><span style="font-size: 18px;"> Instantly reviews customer-submitted text and images to detect profanity, hate speech, nudity, and other brand-risk content using AI models trained on eGifter’s extensive moderation data. It gives merchants real-time protection from inappropriate user-generated content without the lag or cost of manual review queues.</span></li>
</ul>
<blockquote style="padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 20px; margin-left: 0px; font-size: 18px;">
<p>“Consumers love the thoughtfulness that today’s personalization tools make possible,” said <b>Tyler Roye, CEO and co-founder of eGifter</b>. “Brands want to meet that demand with innovation, but they also need cost-effective ways to manage the risks of user-generated content. With eGifter AI Personalization and eGifter AI SmartScreen, you get more creative freedom for shoppers and automated guardrails for merchants.”</p>
</blockquote>
<p style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Both AI tools are live now on </span><a href="https://www.egifter.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><b style="font-size: 18px;">eGifter.com</b></a><span style="font-size: 18px;">, eGifter’s consumer marketplace that also serves as a proving ground for technology before it’s rolled out to merchant partners. Merchants can explore these and other AI-driven tools on the </span><a href="https://corporate.egifter.com/ai-gift-cards/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><b style="font-size: 18px;">eGifter Merchants AI Tools</b></a><span style="font-size: 18px;"> page.</span></p>
<p><b style="font-size: 18px; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);">About eGifter</b></p>
<p style="font-size: 18px;">Founded in 2011, eGifter is a leading gift card technology company powering secure, scalable solutions for consumers, merchants, and businesses. The eGifter ecosystem includes <a href="https://www.egifter.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><b>eGifter.com</b></a> for consumer gift card purchases; <a href="https://egifterrewards.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><b>eGifter Rewards™</b></a> a full-featured platform for enterprise rewards and incentives; <a href="https://express.egifterrewards.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><b>Rewards Express™</b></a> a self-serve option for small business and impromptu rewards; and <a href="https://egiftermerchants.com/?utm_source=eGifter_marketplace" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><b>eGifter Merchants™</b></a>, enabling brands to build and manage gift card programs with white-label storefronts, B2B tools, appeasement solutions, and global payments.
