eGifter announced eGifter Shield, a patent-pending security system using artificial intelligence and machine learning to identify and stop fraudulent activity within gift card programs in real time. The technology analyzes behavioral and technical signals across the gift card lifecycle to intervene during suspicious transactions before value is compromised, with one recent case preventing approximately $250,000 in losses by detecting an attack within seconds.
