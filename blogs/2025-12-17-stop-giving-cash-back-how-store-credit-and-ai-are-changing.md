At a Glance Post-holiday returns are surging, with refund abuse and chargeback scams cutting deep into retail profits. To fight back, smart retailers are replacing cash refunds with store credit and using AI to detect fraud before it happens. Store credit keeps revenue in the ecosystem and buys time to investigate suspicious activity.
